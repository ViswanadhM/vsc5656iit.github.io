About Us

Our journey so far...

  • August 2014

    It all started there!

    Identified that two-wheelers are in India are an underlying pain of many lives.

  • September '14

    Idea > NID > NDBI

    With just an idea we approached professors of NID. We got recommended to NDBI. Who guided us on entering and sustaining a Consumer Product Market.

  • October'14 - December'14

    Feasibility and Customer Research

    We went back to the problem. Took surveys understood and prioritized the problems in the interest of the customer. Performed Root Cause Analysis, QFD, Literature Survey, Market Research and then started Brain storming sessions within the team and experts in the Design field to finally arrive at a possible solution.

  • Jan'15- April'15

    Initial Struggle

    With a Concept in mind, we started approaching Good Product Designers. As with any start-up H.R. has been very tough to handle for us. A lot of struggle went into realizing the design. Though not highly efficient we landed up with very important results regarding the product.

  • May'15-July'15

    Serious Design

    After crossing the initial hiccups, we found right people to work with. We spent lot of time in experimentation. Made Mock-ups. Tested concepts made concept models and finally arrived at a perfect Concept Model.

  • August'15-Now

    Prototyping, MVP and Testing

    Then we started making scaled down models. Went into Engineering Analysis and finally made our first dream come true. We are now testing it to improve it.
